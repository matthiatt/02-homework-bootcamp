Here is a list of specialities I have experience with.

Web Design

Web design is important since it impacts how your audience perceives your brand/image. The impression you make on the consumer can either get them to remain on your page, which would give more oppertunity to sell products and learn about your business or leave your page and turn to a competitor. A good web design will help retain and/or increase the leads on your page.

Development

Website development is a great way to make people aware of the services and/or products you are offering on your website. This is so customers can understand why your products are relevant and/or necessary for them to buy or use. The best way to accomplish this, is to show why your company/product is unique so people can set it apart from competitors.

Optimization

Optimization improves the efficiency of the website at converting visitor traffic into email subscribers, readers, or paying customers.

SEO Optimization

Search engine optimization is a strategy for improving your site's rankings in search engine results. It involves identifying which keywords and phrases your target audience uses when looking for products or services like yours; this includes competitors.

Marketing

The goal of a successful marketing objective is to generate revenue for a brand/company, or organization. Marketing teams achieve this through the execution of strategic digital activities that drive traffic, qualified leads, and sales, in direct collaboration with their sales team.
